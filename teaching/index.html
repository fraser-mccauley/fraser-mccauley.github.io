What you’re paying for

This service is more than homework help or test preparation. I work to instill an intuitive understanding of these topics, and help develop the learning techniques necessary to succeed in future courses. My teaching strategy is based on three pillars:

  1. Identifying the gaps. Using a set of diagnostic tools including collaborative problem solving, I identify the fundamental concepts the student is struggling with. In the hundreds of students I’ve worked with as a tutor and TA, I’ve seen the common pitfalls and points of confusion in these topics. I’ve even experienced many of them myself!

  2. Teaching through dialogue. I design custom lesson plans for each student, to address their specific learning needs. In each session, we work through a series of problems selected to develop conceptual understanding and problem-solving skills. At each step in the process, I make sure the student is able to fully explain their reasoning, and report their solutions in a way that will be satisfactory on tests and assignments.

  3. Practice, practice, practice! The most important part of learning physics and math is independent practice. Following each session, I send the student a set of practice problems curated to reinforce what was discussed. Sometimes these problems will be from external online resources, but in cases where a specific learning gap needs to be addressed, I create custom problem sets and solutions.
