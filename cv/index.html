Summary

As a doctoral researcher in applied optics at Queen’s University’s Ultrafast Laser Lab, I study laser-based manufacturing processes and precision metrology. This research is focused on improving laser welding processes for electric vehicle production. I’m an expert in experimental design, implementation, and analysis for optical material processing and metrology applications. I have experience leading teams, designing experimental apparatus and prototypes for commercial products, and communicating technical information in academic, industrial, governmental, and community outreach settings.
