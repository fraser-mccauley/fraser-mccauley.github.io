I provide virtual and in-person tutoring services for high school students, specializing in mathematics and physics. My focus is on improving my students' critical thinking skills by solving problems together, using visualization through a virtual blackboard to develop an intuitive grasp of abstract concepts. Above all, I create a friendly, supportive learning environment where my students feel comfortable asking questions.

For take-home practice problems and extra resources, I exclusively use Open Educational Resources from either LibreTexts or OpenStax. If I can't find appropriate resources from these collections, I will create them myself.

Pricing

$35/hour for individuals, or $45/hour for groups of up to three.

Group sessions are strongly recommended, as they provide an opportunity for collaborative learning (and for you to save money). Head to my contact page for more information!
